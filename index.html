Steps

  1. Cook rice in a pan of boiling salted according to the packet instructions, then drain and refresh under cold water, leaving it in the colander.
  2. At the same time, poach the fish in a large pam of simmering salted water in a medium heat for 10 minutes, then carefully remove with a slotted spoon. Turn the heat under the poaching water and bring back to a good simmer.
  3. Meanwhile, peel the onions and finely slice with a red chilli (deseed if you like) and coriander stalks. Peel and finely chop the garlic and ginger. Place it all in a large frying pan on a medium heat with 1 tablespoon of oil and the butter, and cook for 10 minutes, or until soft but not coloured, stirring occasionally.
